`ERIS` (Earthquake Risk Intelligence System) is a high-performance **AI-powered Digital Twin** engineered for the **AWS Hackathon**, **Google Solution Challenge 2026**, and advanced seismic intelligence research.  

ERIS bridges the gap between raw environmental sensor data and actionable seismic risk intelligence by combining unconventional earthquake precursors, machine learning, explainable AI, and real-time digital twin visualization.

By monitoring environmental anomalies such as **Radon gas emissions**, **animal behavioral disturbances**, **seismic noise**, **EMF fluctuations**, **groundwater shifts**, and **tectonic movement**, ERIS delivers a multi-layered approach to earthquake forecasting and disaster preparedness.

# 🧠 Core Intelligence (The Brain)

To ensure highly accurate seismic risk prediction, ERIS utilizes advanced machine learning architectures and specialized forecasting logic.

---

## 🔹 Predictive Backbone

### Random Forest Classifier
The initial intelligence engine is powered by a custom-trained `Random Forest Classifier`.

### Innovation Logic

### High Risk (`Risk = 1`) triggers when:
- Radon Levels > 70%
AND
- Animal Anomalies > 70%

OR

- Seismic Noise > 85%

This specialized logic enables ERIS to detect hidden environmental patterns often ignored in conventional systems.

---

## 🔹 Advanced Forecasting Engine

ERIS further expands into hybrid deep learning architectures:

### Hybrid Models
- ConvLSTM
- CNN-LSTM
- Spatial-Temporal Forecasting Networks

These models capture:
- **Where** seismic events may occur
- **When** risk probability increases
